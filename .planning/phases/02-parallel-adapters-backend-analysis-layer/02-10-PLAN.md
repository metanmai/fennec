From RESEARCH B6.5 (HIGH — verified codebase) — flush target = `POST http://127.0.0.1:7821/v1/events` (port 7821 = daemon `LOOPBACK_PORT`). Do NOT add a `/v1/pair` bootstrap endpoint (option b widens the bridge surface — out of scope).
